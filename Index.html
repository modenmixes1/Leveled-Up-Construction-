About Us

Transforming homes and spaces with expert craftsmanship

Your Trusted Construction Partner

At Leveled Up Construction, we believe in transforming spaces into places that inspire. With years of industry experience and a passion for quality craftsmanship, we've built a reputation for excellence in new construction, remodels, additions, and specialized kitchen and bathroom renovations.

Led by Daniel Shuman, our team of skilled professionals is committed to bringing your vision to life while exceeding expectations in quality, timeliness, and attention to detail. We take pride in our work and treat each project as if it were our own home.

From the initial consultation to the final walkthrough, we maintain open communication and transparency throughout the entire process, ensuring your complete satisfaction with the end result.
